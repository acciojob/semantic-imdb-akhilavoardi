Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from within people's dreams. He is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can successfully perform "inception"—planting an idea into someone's subconscious.

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio plays Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt plays Arthur, and Elliot Page plays Ariadne.
