Inception

A science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan.
Actors Quotes
Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from within people's dreams. He is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can successfully perform inception by planting an idea into someone's subconscious mind.

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio plays Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt plays Arthur, Elliot Page plays Ariadne, and Tom Hardy plays Eames.
